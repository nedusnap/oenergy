About Us

Octa Energy Nigeria Limited is a leader in the provision of Technical
and Non-Technical Project Services; Management Manpower, Human Resource
Outsourcing and EPCI to the Oil and Gas industry in Nigeria.
Established in 2017, with strong leadership who have verifiable combined
Industry experience spanning over 30 Years.

Our services vary in scope and complexity ranging from EPCI Project

Management, Engineering and Procurement Solutions, Pipeline Construction

and Repair, Recruitment and Outsourcing Solutions, etc.

We serve companies such as Esso Exploration & Production Nigeria Ltd,

Mobil Producing Nigeria limited, Chevron Nigeria Limited, Shell

Petroleum Development Company and Nigerian Petroleum Development Company Limited

Our Mission

To build a strong engineering service company with focus and passion to promote effectiveness and professionalism in our industry. Our mission is geared towards developing long-term relationship with clients and build a strong corporation that is sensitive, responsive and innovative during providing services and doing business. We carry out our mission with great commitment, professionalism, service and loyalty.
